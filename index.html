7-Minute Bedroom Workout

1. Jumping Jacks (1 minute)

Get your blood pumping and warm up your muscles. Modification: Step side-to-side if jumping isnâ€™t comfortable.

2. Push-Ups (1 minute)

Standard or knee-supported push-ups. Engage your core and maintain a straight body line. Modification: Do incline push-ups against your bed or a sturdy surface.

3. Squats (1 minute)

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower into a squat, and rise back up. Keep your weight in your heels and back straight. Modification: Use your bed for support if needed.

4. Plank (1 minute)

Hold a forearm or full plank position. Keep your core tight and back flat. Modification: Drop to your knees for a supported plank.

5. Bicycle Crunches (1 minute)

Lie on your back, alternate bringing opposite elbows to knees. Engage your abs throughout. Modification: Perform regular crunches if needed.

6. Lunges (30 seconds per leg)

Step forward with one leg, lower until both knees form a 90-degree angle, then switch legs. Modification: Hold onto your bed for balance.

7. Stretch (1 minute)

Cat-Cow Stretch: Alternate between arching and rounding your back while on all fours. Hamstring Stretch: Sit on the floor, reach toward your toes to stretch the back of your legs.

Start your day energized with this quick and easy workout!
